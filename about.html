About GT86

The Toyota 86, also known as the GT86 in Europe and the Scion FR-S in the United States, is a sports car jointly developed by Toyota and Subaru. It features a front-engine, rear-wheel-drive layout and is powered by a 2.0L Subaru Boxer engine.

The design of the 86 pays homage to Toyota's past sports cars, with a sleek and aerodynamic body. The handling is precise and the driving experience is engaging, making it a popular choice among car enthusiasts.

Additional Information

The new GT86 is expected to look similar to the new BRZ given the cars share the same chassis. It’ll follow a clear evolution over the old car’s design, sharing the same basic silhouette, but with sharper features. There’ll be a more chiselled front end, narrower headlamps, a restyled rear bumper and fresh side-skirt vents behind the front wheels.

Despite the car’s engineering development being seemingly complete, a Toyota spokesperson told Auto Express that we’ll have to wait a while before the new GT86 makes it to the UK. This could be due to the differing emissions regulations between the US and Europe, with Toyota potentially reworking the unit’s emission controls to make it EU-compliant.
